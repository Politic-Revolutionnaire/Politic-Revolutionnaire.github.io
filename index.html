What is Science Olympiad?

Science Olympiad is a series of competitions which challenge your intuition, scientific knowledge, and creativity. They are centred on 23 separate challenges ranging from anatomy to astronomy, chemistry to thermodynamics, building projects to quiz events.

Why join?

Science Olympiad is a great opportunity to interact and learn science while working in a team and in a competitive setting.In addition, science olympiad looks great on college applications. In general, it is a fun, yet also productive way to spend your time.

Where do we meet?

Science olympiad meets every Monday for the full hour of enrichment at Mr Bibaud's room.

How do I join?

Just show up!
